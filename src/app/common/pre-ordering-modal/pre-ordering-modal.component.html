About Pre-Ordering

Every confirmed pre-order will be delivered — no exceptions.

A pre-order means reserving your Skye today before it officially ships.

1. Shipping Timeline

New pre-orders are expected to ship starting 30 November 2025. Once your delivery window approaches, we will contact you to confirm your shipping address. The earlier you pre-order, the earlier you will receive your Skye.

2. Special Pre-Order Price

Pre-orders are currently offered at a discounted rate of ₹4,900. This discount is our way of thanking you for supporting our small team and helping us bring Skye to more people.

3. Our Production Model

Skye follows a made-to-order production process to minimize waste and ensure exceptional quality. Production begins once a batch is planned. Regardless of whether we reach our target quantity, every confirmed pre-order will be produced and shipped. If you have placed a pre-order, you can be certain your Skye will be delivered.

4. Refund and Replacement Policy

Due to this production approach, all pre-orders are non-refundable and cannot be canceled once placed. If your Skye arrives defective or damaged, we will provide a free replacement at no cost.

5. Why This Matters

By pre-ordering, you are not only reserving your device, but also supporting a mindful, sustainable production model that reduces waste and ensures a product crafted specifically for you.

6. Stay Updated

We are committed to transparency. Our team will be sharing daily progress updates, so you can follow along as your Skye is brought to life.
