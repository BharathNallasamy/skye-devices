Skye was born as an alternative to the tech monopolies that are fighting more and more aggressively for our time and attention. Technology was supposed to save us time, simplify life, and let us focus on what truly matters, but somehow it has started doing the opposite.

The best engineers in the world work day and night to make you use their technology longer because every extra minute you give them makes them more money. It’s not your fault. You’ve been outnumbered, out-coded, and out-engineered.

Skye exists to give your time back. To reclaim it from technologies designed to keep you hooked. To let you focus, breathe, and live your life fully.

Skye was founded by three friends: Akash, Barath, and Veera. Akash and Barath met during their undergraduate days at Sri Venkateswara College of Engineering in Chennai, India, where they studied Electrical and Electronics Engineering. Akash met Veera during his first job out of college in 2018. That’s how this team came together.

All three of us shared the same frustration. We were noticing how modern tech monopolies shape attention, influence choices, and sometimes make life noisier than it needs to be. The idea of Skye was born in February 2025 after countless conversations about how we could create a solution that actually helps people.

When we first shared our idea, reactions were strong and often mixed. People loved the technology in their lives, but hated how much influence it had over every moment. They wanted control, clarity, and simplicity, but had no clear solution.

So we decided to dig deeper. We ran an online survey with over 700 participants across ages, professions, and genders, asking questions like what usually breaks focus and how people feel when they are distracted too often. The insights we gained were invaluable and helped us understand the real frustrations and needs of our users.

By early May, we started building our first prototype. Testing it across demographics gave us amazing feedback, and we knew we were on the right track. Soon after, we began working on the production version.

We want Skye to be durable, reliable, and responsible. E-waste is already a huge problem worldwide, and we refuse to add to it unnecessarily. That’s why we removed the USB-C charging cable. Most people already have one, and Skye only needs to be charged once a week. Fewer cables, less waste, and less packaging.

The components we use are the best available worldwide, and our PCBs are custom-made in-house, making Skye a product you can trust to last.

We also wanted Skye to be something more people could own. We wanted everyone to have a chance to save themselves from the tech monopolies that steal their time. So we’ve kept the price super honest, considering the high-end materials and technology we use.

Skye is being rolled out in a made-to-order, pre-order fashion. This means we produce only the essential amount, keeping things economically and environmentally responsible, while making the product accessible to everyone who truly needs it.

Skye is just the beginning of something bigger. It’s a shift toward slow, mindful, and intentional living. Thank you for taking the time to understand our project, our vision, and our story.
